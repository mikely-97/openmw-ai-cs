================================================================================
1. GAME OVERVIEW
================================================================================

Jungle Troll Tribes is a multiplayer team survival/crafting game set in a 
hostile jungle. Players control troll heroes who must:
- Gather resources (wood, stone, herbs, animal parts)
- Craft items, weapons, armor, potions, and buildings
- Manage survival stats (Food, Heat, Energy, Health)
- Fight hostile beasts and boss creatures
- Build a village with their team
- Complete quests from the Old Troll NPC

Version: 7.0.2 (5 January 2026)
Website: www.jungletrolls.com  
Author: A.Domin (troleplayers)
Discord: https://discord.com/invite/q4SNMYfV3E

Map Type: Team survival/crafting (3 teams)
Day-Night Cycle: 500 seconds per full cycle (~8.3 minutes)
One "week" in-game: ~58 minutes real time

GAME MODES (selected via chat command at start):
  -action    Action mode (combat-focused)
  -plenty    Plenty mode (abundant resources)
  -tutorial  Tutorial mode (guided gameplay)
  -easy      Easy difficulty
  -normal    Normal difficulty (default)
  -hard      Hard difficulty
  -debug     Fix item limit bugs


================================================================================
2. TROLL CLASSES (4 Hero Types)
================================================================================

Each player picks one of 4 troll classes. Each has unique abilities, recipes,
and stat bonuses:

WARRIOR (Unit ID: H003)
  Role: Melee combat specialist
  Special: "Warrior Modes" (stance switching for offense/defense)
  Unique Recipes: 24 class-specific (heavy weapons, shields, armor upgrades)
  Weapon Focus: Daggers, spears, axes, clubs, mauls, swords
  Abilities: Power attacks, shield bash, berserker rage

HUNTER (Unit ID: H001)
  Role: Ranged specialist with trapping and animal taming
  Special: Pet system (Hunter's Dog companion)
    - Dog takes 75% damage from beasts
    - Dog takes 120% damage from Hunter Troll
    - Dog takes 100% from other trolls and Boss Beasts
  Unique Recipes: 13 class-specific (bows, traps, tents, pet dens)
  Weapon Focus: Bows, longbows, composite bows, arrows
  Stat Bonus: Max Food 250 (vs normal 200)
  Damage Profile: 100% to trolls, 120% to beasts, 50% to buildings

SHAMAN (Unit ID: H002)
  Role: Magic/potion specialist
  Special: Alchemy leveling system (Alchemy_Lvl trigger)
  Unique Recipes: 15 class-specific (wands, potions, elixirs, voodoo)
  Weapon Focus: Wands, staves, voodoo dolls
  Abilities: Hexes, curses, healing, buff spells

INVENTOR (Unit ID: H000)
  Role: Engineering/technology specialist
  Special: Can craft machines, explosives, advanced buildings
  Unique Recipes: 11 class-specific + workshop recipes
  Weapon Focus: Guns, shotguns, chainsaws, remote mines, bombs
  Stat Bonus: Max Heat 125 (vs normal 100)
  Crafts: Engines, watchtowers, siege equipment, stockage boxes

HERO STATS:
  - Strength → +1 health per point
  - Agility → combat speed / dodge
  - Intelligence → mana / spell power  
  - Health regeneration: +0.01 per stat point
  - Heroes gain XP from kills and quests
  - Death counter tracked (affects score/achievements)


================================================================================
3. SURVIVAL MECHANICS
================================================================================

3.1 FOOD / HUNGER SYSTEM
  - Stat range: 0-200 (Hunter max: 250)
  - Decreases over time automatically
  - Low Food → "Starving" → continuous health damage
  - At 0 Food → rapid health loss, can die
  - Restore by eating: raw meat, cooked food, berries, herbs, potions
  
  FOOD TYPES (from udg_Eaten_* tracking variables):
  - Raw Meat → basic food, low value, risk of disease
  - Cooked Stew → better food value, requires campfire/cauldron
  - Berries → basic food gathered from bushes
  - Honey → food + minor healing
  - Egg/Omelette → moderate food value
  - Plants/Herbs → herbal food
  - Fish → from fishing, can be cooked
  - Alcohol/Strong Liquor → drink with side effects
  - Potions → various liquid consumables
  - Troll Meat → cannibalism mechanic (special consequences)
  - Human Meat → forbidden food (special consequences)
  - Old Troll's Cake → quest reward food
  - Bone/Fishbone → very low quality, emergency food
  - Bad food → can cause Vomiting (udg_Eaten_Vomit)

3.2 HEAT / WARMTH SYSTEM
  - Stat range: 0-100 (Inventor max: 125)
  - Decreases at night (faster in snow/rain)
  - "Wet" status accelerates heat loss dramatically
  - Thresholds:
    * Below ~70 → minor speed/power penalty
    * Below 50 → "Frozen" status: slow movement + health loss
    * Prolonged cold → "Fever" disease
  - Heat Restoration:
    * Stand near campfire/fire pit
    * Carry a lit torch
    * Be inside buildings/shelters
    * Wear warm clothing (fur coats, lined armor)
    * Hot springs on the map

3.3 ENERGY SYSTEM
  - Mana-like resource for abilities and actions
  - Regenerates over time
  - At 0 Energy → "Coma" status: lose heat, regenerate energy,
    until full energy or attacked
  - Used by: active weapon abilities, spells, crafting actions

3.4 HEALTH
  - Standard hit points
  - Slow passive regeneration
  - +1 max HP per Strength point
  - Healing sources: food, potions, Shaman healing, rest near fire
  - Death → respawn (with death counter penalty)

3.5 DISEASES
  - Malaria (buff B00I) → from jungle mosquitoes/environment
  - Plague (buff B00H) → from certain creatures/contamination
  - Fever (buff Brej) → from prolonged cold exposure
  - Cured by: Antidote potions, Shaman abilities

3.6 DAY-NIGHT CYCLE (500 seconds per cycle)
  - Day: Safer, heat stable, better visibility
  - Night: Dangerous, heat drops fast, predators more active
  - Day counter (udg_Day) tracks progression
  - Old Troll announces: "Today be day X"
  - Boss spawns tied to day progression (periodic boss events)
  - Snow/weather events at certain times


================================================================================
4. RESOURCE SYSTEM
================================================================================

4.1 BASE RESOURCES (gathered from environment nodes)
  - Wood (I011) → from trees, primary building/crafting material
  - Stone (I014) → from rock nodes, tools and buildings
  - Flint (I08T) → fire-starting, black powder ingredient
  - Oil (I013) → fuel, lubricant, crafting ingredient
  - Tinder → fire-starting material
  - Rope → crafted from plant fibers, universal binding material
  
4.2 HERBS AND PLANTS
  - Berries → food, Berry Seeds craftable
  - Herb varieties (4 named herbs used in alchemy):
    * Aria → potion ingredient
    * Bardane → potion ingredient
    * Corian → potion ingredient
    * Draximor → potion ingredient
  - Mana Stone → magical crafting component
  - Seeds → plantable (Berry, Tree, Herb seeds)
  - Petrified Wood → rare/special wood variant

4.3 ANIMAL DROPS (from hunting/killing)
  By Animal Type:
  
  PIGS/BOARS:
  - Raw Meat, Pig Skin, Bone
  
  BEARS:
  - Raw Meat, Bear Fur, Bone
  
  SABRETOOTH (big cats):
  - Raw Meat, Sabretooth Fur, Bone
  
  RABBITS:
  - Raw Meat, Rabbit Fur
  
  RACOONS:
  - Raw Meat, Racoon Fur
  
  ANTELOPES:
  - Raw Meat, Antelope Leather, Antelope Horns
  
  ALLIGATORS/CROCODILES:
  - Raw Meat, Alligator Scales/Leather
  
  SNAKES:
  - Raw Meat, Snake Scales/Leather, Venom
  
  CRABS:
  - Raw Meat, Crab Chitin
  
  SPIDERS:
  - Spider Silk, Spider Venom/Poison
  
  BIRDS:
  - Feathers (I00F), Eggs, Raw Meat
  
  FISH (via fishing):
  - Fish, Fish Bone (I00E)
  
  SPECIAL DROPS:
  - Skull → from trolls/bosses
  - Shrunken Head → rare voodoo material
  - Giant Egg → from boss creatures
  - Giant Shell → from giant tortoise boss
  - Horns → from horned creatures
  - Hearts → rare powerful food/alchemy ingredient

4.4 PROCESSED MATERIALS
  - Bronze Bar → smelted at Forge (copper ore + tin ore or equivalent)
  - Iron Bar → smelted at Forge (iron ore + fuel)
  - Linen/Cloth → processed from plant fibers
  - Leather variants → tanned at Tannery from raw hides
  - Black Powder → from Flint (Inventor recipe)
  - Fuel → from Oil (Inventor recipe)
  - Parchment → writing material for plans/recipes

4.5 FISHING SYSTEM
  - Requires crafted Fishing Rod
  - Needs Bait (craftable: "some Fishin' Bait" is a Basic recipe)
  - Catches fish for cooking
  - Fish Bones as crafting byproduct
  - Check_and_Cast_Fishing_Rod trigger handles mechanics

4.6 ANIMAL REPRODUCTION
  - 4 separate reproduction triggers (Animal_Reproduction_1-4)
  - Animals respawn over time in their habitat zones
  - Ecosystem balance: overhunting reduces future spawns
  - Different animal types have different reproduction rates

================================================================================
6. BUILDINGS & STRUCTURES
================================================================================

Buildings are crafted as "kits" (portable items), then placed on the map.
Construction uses a progress bar system (Building_ProgressBar trigger).
Once finished, buildings gain abilities (Finish_Building_Global trigger).

BUILDING TYPES (identified from recipes and code):

6.1 BASIC BUILDINGS (all classes)
  - Camp Fire → basic warmth source, cooking station
    Recipe: varies (basic materials)
  - Merchant Shop → NPC trading post
    Recipe: 1 Stone + 3 Wood → Shop kit (I03C)
  
6.2 HUNTER BUILDINGS  
  - Pet's Den → houses Hunter's pet companion
    Recipe: 2 Wood + 1 Stone
  - Hunter's Tent (3 variants):
    * Bear Fur Tent → Recipe: Bear Fur + Wood + Rope
    * Sabretooth Fur Tent → Recipe: Sabretooth Fur + Wood + Rope
    * Pig Skin Tent → Recipe: Pig Skin + Wood + Rope
  - Sensor Trap → detects approaching enemies
  - Spiked Trap → damage trap for enemies
  - Rock Trap → area damage trap
  - Ensnare Trap → immobilizes enemies

6.3 INVENTOR BUILDINGS
  - Stockage Box → storage container
    Recipe: 1 Wood
  - Watchtower → defensive structure, can be upgraded
    (Upgrade_Tower trigger handles tower upgrades)
  - Workshop → enables Workshop recipes
  - Siege equipment → advanced military structures

6.4 CRAFT BUILDINGS (from Workshop recipes)
  - Armory → weapon and armor crafting station
  - Tannery → leather/hide processing
  - Forge → metal smelting (Bronze/Iron Bars)
  - Cauldron → potion brewing station
  - Voodoo Hut → Shaman magical crafting

6.5 BUILDING COMBAT
  - Buildings have HP and can be destroyed
  - Building damage reduction: 33% from trolls, 10% from beasts
  - Buildings can be equipped with weapons (towers)
  - Building_Death trigger handles destruction
  - Buildings can use equipped weapons or special abilities

  PIGS/BOARS

  BEARS
  - Dangerous prey, drops: Raw Meat, Bear Fur, Bone
  - Found in forest/mountain areas
  
  SABRETOOTH CATS
  - Dangerous predator, drops: Raw Meat, Sabretooth Fur, Bone
  
  RABBITS
  - Easy prey, drops: Raw Meat, Rabbit Fur
  - Quick and hard to catch
  
  RACOONS  
  - Small game, drops: Raw Meat, Racoon Fur
  
  ANTELOPES
  - Medium prey, drops: Raw Meat, Antelope Leather, Antelope Horns
  
  ALLIGATORS/CROCODILES
  - Dangerous aquatic predator, drops: Raw Meat, Alligator Scales
  - Found near water
  
  SNAKES
  - Venomous, drops: Raw Meat, Snake Scales, Venom
  - Can cause poison/disease
  
  CRABS
  - Coastal creature, drops: Raw Meat, Crab Chitin
  
  SPIDERS
  - Venomous, drops: Spider Silk, Spider Venom
  - Tarantula variant (larger, more dangerous)
  
  BIRDS/VULTURES
  - Drops: Feathers, Eggs, Raw Meat
  
  FISH
  - Caught via Fishing Rod + Bait
  - Drops: Fish, Fish Bone
  
  WOLVES

    * Wolf_Swallow_JASS → wolf can swallow items/prey
    * Wolf_Swallow_Damage__JASS → damage from swallowed items
    * Wolf_Die → special death mechanics

  TORTOISES
  - Tough creature, drops: Giant Shell (for Tortoise Armor/Shield)

7.2 ANIMAL REPRODUCTION
  - 4 reproduction zones/triggers
  - Animals respawn periodically in their habitat
  - Color variants possible (Animal_Color trigger)

7.3 BOSS CREATURES

  Boss 1: WEREWOLF (Unit: n01E)

  - HP set to 1000
  - Super creep spawn sound
  
  Boss 4: GIANT VULTURE (Unit: n01L)
  - Spawns in: Giant_Vulture region
  
  Additional Boss-related:
  - Boss[1-4] flags track which bosses are alive
  - Mojo Anaconda and Mojo Tarantula → special quest bosses
  - Boss attacks deal 100% to trolls/beasts, 33% to buildings
  - Bosses drop rare/unique items

8. QUEST SYSTEM
================================================================================

Quests are given by the Old Troll NPC (unit h00F).
New quests spawn based on day progression (New_quest trigger).
The Old Troll's user data tracks quest cooldown.

QUEST TYPES (from code analysis):

  Quest: "Bring Berries and Strong Liquor"
  - Old Troll says: "Me kidz, this old troll here needz something sweet.
    Bring some berries and strong liquor and I'll teach ya a good ol' recipe
    how cook 'em!"
  - Reward: Cooking recipe

  Quest: "Hot Spring Discovery"
  - Reveals Hot Spring locations (north and south)
  - Minimap pings show locations
  - Hot Springs provide heat restoration

  Quest: "Mojo Anaconda / Mojo Tarantula" 
  - Kill special boss creatures
  - Items placed at Mojo Anaconda and Mojo Tarantula regions
  - Minimap pings guide players

  Additional Quests (from TRIGSTR references):
  - TRIGSTR_1047 → quest announcement
  - TRIGSTR_897 → quest announcement
  - TRIGSTR_687 → quest announcement
  - TRIGSTR_996 → quest announcement
  - TRIGSTR_1594 → quest about Mojo creatures
  - TRIGSTR_2826 → quest announcement
  
  Quest rewards include: unique recipes, rare items, map reveals

  ACHIEVEMENTS system (Achievements trigger):
  - Track player accomplishments
  - Likely tied to kills, crafting, survival milestones


================================================================================
9. COMBAT SYSTEM
================================================================================

9.1 DAMAGE TYPES AND MULTIPLIERS
  Troll/Boss attacks:  100% to trolls/beasts, 33% to buildings
  Beast attacks:       100% to trolls/beasts, 10% to buildings  
  Hunter attacks:      100% to trolls, 120% to beasts, 50% to buildings
  Power Hit (ability): 100% to trolls/beasts, 10% to buildings
  Special:             100% to units, 200% to buildings (siege)

9.2 WEAPON CATEGORIES (from ability triggers)
  Melee:
  - Daggers → fast, low damage
  - Spears → medium range thrust
  - Axes → medium damage, can throw
  - Clubs (Bone, Spiked, Big) → slow, high damage
  - Mauls → very slow, very high damage
  - Swords → balanced
  - Chainsaws (Inventor) → high sustained damage

  Ranged:
  - Bows (basic, Long, Composite) → arrow-based ranged
  - Guns/Shotguns (Inventor) → ammo-based ranged
  - Javelins → thrown spears
  - Throwing Axes → thrown axes
  - Harpoons (HARPOON_ID = I0AG) → special thrown weapon

  Magical:
  - Wands → Shaman magic attacks
  - Staves → enhanced Shaman weapons
  - Voodoo Dolls → curse-based attacks
  - SpellBooks → collected spell compendiums

  Off-hand:
  - Shields (Bone, Tortoise, Horned, Ironwood, Giant)
  - Torches → light source + fire damage

9.3 AMMO SYSTEM
  - Arrows → for Bows (Ammo_add trigger)
  - Poisoned Arrows (4 types):
    * Spider Poisoned Arrows
    * Snake Poisoned Arrows
    * Tarantula Poisoned Arrows
    * Envenomed Arrows
  - Ammo/Black Powder → for Guns (Inventor)
  - Fuel → for certain Inventor weapons
  - Ammo_poison trigger handles poison application

9.4 ARMOR SYSTEM
  Leather Armors (by material):
  - Pig Skin Coat → basic leather
  - Bear Fur Coat → warm, good defense
  - Sabretooth Fur Coat → premium fur
  - Racoon Fur Coat → light fur
  - Rabbit Fur Coat → lightest fur
  - Snake Leather Coat → scaled
  - Alligator Leather Coat → heavy scaled
  - Crab Chitin Coat → crustacean shell
  
  Lined variants → fur + lining for extra warmth
  Bronze variants → metal-reinforced leather
  Iron variants → heavy metal armor
  Tortoise Armor → giant shell-based

  Boots (by material, same animal types):
  - Basic, Lined, Bronze variants
  - Boots of Haste → speed bonus
  
  Gauntlets (same material system):
  - Basic, Lined, Bronze variants  
  - Gauntlets of Fury → attack bonus

  Headpieces:
  - Voodoo Masks (multiple variants):
    * Basic Voodoo Mask
    * Magical Voodoo Mask (+ Mana Stone + Giant Egg/Horns/Shrunken Head)
    * Petrified Voodoo Mask
    * Demonic Voodoo Mask
    * Ghostly Voodoo Mask
    * Voodoo Mask of Life
  - Helmets (from Armory)

9.5 IMBUING / ENCHANTING
  - Gauntlets can be imbued → "of Fury" variant (attack bonus)
  - Boots can be imbued → "of Haste" variant (speed bonus)
  - Requires: item + Mana Stone + Feather
  - 34 imbuing recipes covering all material types
  - Staves and Wands can also be imbued (38 recipes)


================================================================================
10. ADDITIONAL SYSTEMS
================================================================================

10.1 SPELLBOOK SYSTEM
  - 29 SpellBook recipes
  - SpellBooks combine multiple spells into one item
  - Shaman-focused magical progression
  - Spells include: Fireball, Lightning, Teleport, Freeze, Blink,
    Plague, Malaria, Vanish, Mirror Image, Double Image

10.2 TANNERY SYSTEM (58 recipes)
  - Converts raw animal hides into usable leather/fur
  - Processes all animal skin types:
    * Pig Skin → Pig Leather
    * Bear Fur → processed fur
    * Sabretooth Fur → premium processed fur
    * Racoon/Rabbit Fur → light processed fur
    * Snake/Alligator/Crab → scaled leather/chitin
  - Lined variants add warmth bonus
  - Bronze-plated variants add armor bonus

10.3 INVENTION PLANS SYSTEM (9 plans)
  - Plans for an Engine
  - Recipe for Greatest Elixir
  - Plans for a Mecha-Golem
  - Plans for Harpoons
  - Plans for a Giant Shield
  - Plans for Fiery Axes
  - Plans for Thunder Clubs
  - Plans for a Chainsaw
  - A devious plan (trap/sabotage?)
  Plans are crafted at Workshop, then used to create the final item.

10.4 CRAFT INVENTION SYSTEM
  - Crafting invented items from plans
  - Consumes plan + materials
  - Creates: Chainsaw, Harpoons, Giant Shield, etc.
  - Team announcements when crafted (e.g., "We haz crafted a Chainsaw!")

10.5 ITEM STACKING & DISASSEMBLY
  - Items can be stacked (Stacking triggers)
  - Items can be disassembled back to components (8+ disassembly recipes)
  - Allows resource recovery from unwanted items

10.6 TROLL MERCHANT
  - NPC shop building
  - Buy/sell items for shell tokens or resources
  - Recipe_Troll_Merchant handles trades

10.7 MULTIBOARD (scoreboard)
  - Create_MultiBoard trigger
  - Displays team scores, day count, kill counts
  - Tracks player stats and achievements

10.8 TELEPORTER SYSTEM
  - Use_teleporter trigger
  - Teleport between specific map locations
  - Requires specific items or buildings

10.9 VOTING SYSTEM
  - Voting trigger for team decisions
  - Used for game mode selection or team choices

10.10 WEAPON/ARMOR EQUIP SYSTEM
  - Weapon_and_offHand__Gain → equipping weapons/shields
  - Weapon_and_offHand__Lose → unequipping
  - ArmorBootGauntlet__Lose → removing armor
  - Headpiece_Lose → removing headgear
  - SpellBookVoodooDoll__Lose → removing spellbooks/dolls
  - Auto-applies stat bonuses on equip, removes on unequip
